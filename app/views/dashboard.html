Check out the awesome apps available from FFXIV Organizer




Inventory

The inventory app is designed to model your inventory in FFXIV Online. Search for items through the database app to add to your inventory. See if you have stock to create items using the lists app!


Lists

With the lists app you can make lists of items you want to craft and see which items you will need to craft them. Sign up for the inventory app and see your current stock displayed! Use the database app to add items to your lists!

Database

A simple database with all the information needed to make quick simple lists. Search for items to add to lists of items you wish to craft. Find items to add to your inventory. Updated for FFXIV version 4.4!
